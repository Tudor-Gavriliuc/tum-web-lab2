Materials

At Custom Doors, we take pride in using only the finest materials to craft doors that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also durable and long-lasting. Our selection of materials is carefully curated to ensure superior quality, functionality, and sustainability. We specialize in solid wood doors, which offer timeless beauty, excellent insulation, and durability. We work with premium hardwoods like oak, mahogany, maple, and walnut, providing a natural and warm aesthetic for any home. For a more cost-effective yet sturdy option, we also offer engineered wood doors, which provide excellent resistance to warping and cracking. For modern and contemporary designs, we incorporate glass paneling, using tempered, frosted, or stained glass to enhance style while maintaining security and privacy. We also craft steel and aluminum doors, which provide superior strength, security, and weather resistance, making them ideal for exterior applications. Additionally, our doors are finished with high-quality veneers, paints, and stains, ensuring longevity and a flawless appearance. We prioritize eco-friendly materials and finishes, ensuring that our craftsmanship aligns with sustainable practices. Whatever your style or preference, we offer custom solutions tailored to your needs with materials designed to stand the test of time.
